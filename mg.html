About

MG LEGACY

Tracing its history back to 1924, MG is the iconic British motoring brand,
famous for building sporty and exciting cars which are always fun to drive
from the original MF 14/28 Super Sports car, designed by legendary cecil
kimber, to the all-electric MG ZS EV of today, MG has always been innovative,
always been radical and always been fun!

Today,MG is the fastest growing car brand in the world, fielding a product
range from hatchbacks,sedans, SUVs. Designed in Marylebone, London.
and manufactured on state-of-the-art factories in several countries today's MGs
are practical, spacious, packed with technology perfect for modern life.
